Our Music

Pop-Punk Anthems

Get ready for infectious melodies, high-energy vocals, and lyrics that speak to your soul. We spin the best of classic pop-punk and the freshest tracks from the new wave, guaranteed to make you jump and sing along!

Hard-Hitting Rock

From the raw power of garage rock to the intricate solos of progressive rock, our rock selection is diverse and impactful. Expect a mix of iconic tracks and groundbreaking new artists that keep the spirit of rock alive and thriving.

Unleashed Metal

Prepare for sonic assaults and intricate shredding! We delve into various subgenres of metal, bringing you everything from thrash and death metal to metalcore and progressive metal. This is where the heavy truly gets heavy.
